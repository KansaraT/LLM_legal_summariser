
**Court Orders Inquiry into Long-Standing Encroachment on Village Land**

- The case pertains to alleged encroachment on Gaon Sabha (village council) land that continued for over 20 years without authorities taking preventive action.

- The court directed the Principal Secretary (Revenue) to personally inquire into the matter within 4 weeks. 

- The inquiry must ascertain liability of officials responsible for allowing the encroachment and identify proposed action against negligent officials.

- The Collector of Sitapur must submit an affidavit regarding preliminary inquiry findings within 2 weeks after completion of the 4 week inquiry period.

- The case will be listed again on 12th October 2022 for submission of the inquiry results affidavit.
